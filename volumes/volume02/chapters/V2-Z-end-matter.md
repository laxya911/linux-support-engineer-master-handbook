# Build Your Lab

The only way to truly master advanced administration is to break it. We strongly recommend expanding your home lab.

To take it further:
1. **Try Proxmox**: Spin up multiple VMs and configure a virtual private network between them.
2. **Break Things on Purpose**: Misconfigure an LVM volume, block your own SSH access with a firewall rule—then try to recover from the console.
